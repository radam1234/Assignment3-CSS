    The Sweet Journey of Hershey's

    Hershey's was founded by Milton S. Hershey in 1894 and is one of the largest chocolate manufacturers in the world.

    Interesting Facts

    • - Hershey became interested in chocolate after the 1893 World's Fair in Chicago. Inspired by German chocolate production, he opened the Hershey Chocolate Company in 1900, selling his caramel
    • - In response to a potential threat from Beech Nut Gum, Hershey introduced "Easy Chew" gum in 1915. Import restrictions on sugar and chicle led to the discontinuation of Easy Chew in 1924
    • - Hershey's KISSES debuted in 1907, but the origin of the name remains a mystery. One theory suggests the name might be related to the sound or motion during manufacturing
    • - KISSES were initially wrapped by hand until wrapping machines were developed in 1921.
    • - Hershey built the town of Hershey, Pennsylvania, for the well-being of his factory employees. The community included brick houses, lawns, and Hersheypark for recreational purposes
    • - The company's logo was the "Cocoa Bean Baby" from 1898 until 1968 when it became the Hershey Food Corporation.
